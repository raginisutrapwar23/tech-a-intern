Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • All-Purpose Flour (maida)
  • Ghee (or oil)
  • Ghee (or oil for greasing)
  • Milk (use more as needed)
  • Curd (yogurt or 3/4 tbsp lemon juice)
  • Baking Soda (or 1/8 th tsp)
  • Ghee (or oil for deep frying)

Recipe

  1. Prepare the sugar syrup
  2. Make the dough for Gulab Jamun
  3. Shape the dough in small balls
  4. Soak the fried balls in Gulab Jamun
  5. Garnish with dry fruits

Dhokla

Ingredients

  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Fine Semolina (fine suji/rava)
  • Turmeric
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Lemon Juice (or lime juice or 1/3 teaspoon natural citric acid)
  • Oil
  • Ginger Paste

Recipe

  1. Mix all the ingredients (except Eno powder) to form a smooth batter.
  2. Allow the batter to ferment for 1-2 hours.
  3. Grease a tray and pour the batter into it.
  4. Sprinkle some red chili powder on top.
  5. Steam until the dhokla is soft and fluffy.
  6. Cut into squares and serve with green chutney or tamarind chutney.
